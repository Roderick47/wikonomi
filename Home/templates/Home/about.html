Welcome

Hi, living in PNG is tough, the cost of living and prices of goods and services are going up. But what if with a click of a button you could find the cheapest and best products?

As way to help Papua New Guineans I built this site. Now you can find the best products and services at the best prices with ease.
But we need your help to make this platform work.

How can you help?

The best way to help is to use this website as intended.

  1. Search for products that you want.
  2. Edit/curate the information of the product. if it is correct. Verify it again.
  3. If the product is not listed. get it listed by posting it yourself.
  4. Recommend the site to your friends so they can use it.
